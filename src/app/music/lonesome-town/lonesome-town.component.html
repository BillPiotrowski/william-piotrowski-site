Lonesome Town Single by Ryan Ross and Z Berg

Description

Within a month of the Covid lockdowns first taking place in 2020, the air quality in Los Angeles became the cleanest it has been in decades. From my street, I saw mountains that I had never seen before. A result of us individually doing our part to protect each other.

As the pandemic continued on and events cast a spotlight toward the hatred and injustice that exists in the world, I was reminded of that power we all have as individuals.

During that early phase of the lockdowns, Ryan Ross and I produced the arrangement for this cover of Lonesome Town featuring Z Berg on vocals. As proud as I am of the music, I am more proud that it is complemented by a video assembled from clips created by young artists all around the globe.

A lot of small acts can have a great affect.
