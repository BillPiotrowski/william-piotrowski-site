Endless Night Single by William Piotrowski

Description

In 2019, I was asked to compose a song to be sung by the talented Ariel de Ment to be performed at the Endless Night Vampire Ball in New Orleans on Halloween. The concept was to show the transition of a woman into a vampire as a decision, not as a victim.

Details

  • Performed Live on 10.31.2018
  • Vocals by Ariel de Ment
