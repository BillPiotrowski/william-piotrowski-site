What Separates Us Original Motion Picture Soundtrack by William Piotrowski

Description

What Separates Us is my second score for a feature film and gave me an opportunity to explore a palette of new sounds while still maintaining dark and somber overtones. The film centers around a group of young men growing up in a poor part of Helena, Montana. The concept for the score was to create a sound that was sad and yet still remained hopeful. Like a melody heard emanating from the distant Irish Catholic cathedral that looms over the city.

Details
