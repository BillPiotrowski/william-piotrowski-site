Grey Cardinal Single by Collapsing Scenery (ft. Ryan Ross)

Description

Most remixes are faster and more club-ready than the original. Not this one. Ryan and I took this as an opportunity to slow down the original Collapsing Scenery track and accompany Mickey's haunting vocals and lyrics with pagan strings, flutes and percussion. The concept was based on imagery of The Witch (2015, Robert Eggers) and Midsommar (Ari Aster, 2019). This track gave me a chance to go back to my roots with a darker sound as well as continue to develop sampling techniques. During the mixing phase, I had the pleasure to meet and work closely with Don of Collapsing Scenery, who encouraged Ryan and I to trust our instincts with the sampling and mix process.
